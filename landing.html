01

Who This Is For

We're doing this work because we love our community and deeply believe that when queer people thrive economically, the world around us does too. And it's not just speculation, there's actually a lot of data that shows when women and other minority groups gain economic advantage, they do more for their communities with their wealth than non-marginalized folks. We believe the philosophy that underlies queer culture is one that can change the world for the better, and that when 2sLGBTQIA+ people are able to be heard and celebrated culturally and economically, everyone benefits.

You've probably built something already — maybe multiple somethings. You have skills, experience, and work you're proud of. But somewhere between having the talent and building the business, the typical playbook stops making sense.

You might have noticed that most business advice assumes you want to be neutral, palatable, or "professional" in ways that erase the very things that make your work interesting. You may have tried to follow the templates and felt like you were disappearing in the process.

This community is for 2sLGBTQIA+ entrepreneurs and creatives who refuse to flatten themselves for market fit — and who want to learn how to thrive without sacrificing what makes them whole.

02

What This Isn't

Differently rejects the notion that you can copy and paste brand and business strategies across people. In a lot of professional spaces the word leadership can carry a connotation of adopting a professional persona that has little to do with who you actually are. The goal is to fit into existing templates rather than building something that reflects your full complexity. Often that goal is created by the business owner offering services because there's a culture of thinking that if it worked for me this way, it will work for you.

We do not offer a one size fits all solution, or a quick fix for your business. This space is built in the spirit of discovering what makes each of us shine, with the understanding that the combination of things that add up to a brand image that feels right will be different for each of us. Leadership, community, and connectedness are the skills that will help us to see and celebrate these things about ourselves and get them out into the world.

This isn't about learning to code-switch better or package yourself for mass appeal. It's about building economic power while staying whole — which requires different strategies, frameworks, and honest conversations about what it actually takes.

This Is Not
  • Business advice that ignores identity
  • Templates that require you to be palatable
  • Get-rich-quick schemes or growth hacking
  • A passive course library you consume alone
  • A place where you need to tone yourself down
This Is
  • Strategy that centers your full identity
  • Building power without erasing yourself
  • Real mentorship and daily founder presence
  • A community that gets the unique challenges we face
  • A room where complexity is welcomed, not flattened
